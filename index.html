About Us

Co-op! is a company which publishes crowd-sourced reviews about different co-op and internship positions for universities across America. The site has pages devoted to individual companies, where users can submit a review of their experience and a rating from one to five stars for a specific position after verification. In order to maintain the integrity of our site, we require users to sign up for an account before viewing reviews and require a file upload including proof of employment at a company.

Join Our Growing Network

Co-op! currently has reviews for over 100 different positions at over 35 companies across the US. Our reviewers come from different backgrounds, ranging from current sophomores enrolled in a university co-op program to graduating seniors looking to gain more professional experience before graduation.
